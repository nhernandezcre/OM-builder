HELP & INSTRUCTIONS

Getting Started

Click any text field to start editing. Placeholder text clears automatically when you click in, and returns if you leave the field empty. Use Tab to move between fields in logical order.

Set up your broker profile first (Menu → My Profile). Your name, contact info, and headshot will auto-fill into the cover, company profile, and back cover for every OM you create.

Text Formatting

Click any editable field to see the floating format bar: A- / A+ to adjust font size, L C R J for left, center, right, or justify alignment.

For the Investment Summary and Market Summary, paste your full write-up into the text block. These are single-field paste targets, not individual paragraphs.

Photos & Drag-and-Drop

Open the photo tray (Menu → Photos), then click + Add Photos to bulk-upload images. Drag any thumbnail from the tray onto a placeholder on the page. A preview follows your cursor and the target highlights in teal.

You can also click any placeholder directly to upload a single image from your computer.

Once placed, hover over an image for controls: Zoom (+/-) and Position arrows to pan. Zoom out to see the full uncropped image. The cover photo automatically applies as a darkened background on all section divider pages.

Rent Roll

Click the Type column to select from a preset list (Studio, 1bd/1ba, etc.) or choose Custom to type your own. Use + Add Unit Row to add rows. Hover any row to see the delete button (×).

All financial fields auto-format on blur: type "1500" and it becomes "$1,500". Rents, expenses, and other income all feed into the auto-calculation engine.

Sales Comparables

Comp cards auto-calculate $/Unit, $/SF, and GRM when you enter Price, Units, Bldg SF, and GSR. Double-click the Date field to open a date picker. Use + Add Comp for more cards.

Maps

The Aerial Map and Comp Map pages have placeholder zones for map screenshots. Take a screenshot from Google Maps (or any map tool) and drag it in from the photo tray. Add or remove amenity/comp entries with the +/× buttons.

Page Visibility

Not every deal needs every page. Hover any content page to see the eye icon in the top-right. Click to hide it from the PDF. Hidden pages appear dimmed on screen but won't print. Click again to restore.

Exporting to PDF

Click Download PDF. In Chrome's print dialog, set:

Landscape • Margins: None • Background graphics: On

Then click "Save as PDF". Hidden pages will not appear in the export.

Saving & Loading

Your work auto-saves every 2 seconds to your browser. If you close and reopen, click Load Last Save from the menu. Reset clears the deal content but keeps your broker profile. Use Undo to roll back to a snapshot taken every 30 seconds (up to 10 steps).
